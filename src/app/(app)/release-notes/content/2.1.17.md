improved: []
fixed:
  - title: "Merchant page showed the wrong merchant's outlets"
    detail: "When expanding outlets on the merchant page, the lookup matched the URL identifier (a merchant fid) against the merchant's surrogate primary key as well as its fid and external id. Because fids and primary keys share the same numeric space, a fid could collide with a different merchant's primary key — and that match would win, returning the wrong merchant's outlets. For example, the merchant with fid 964 was shown outlets belonging to merchant_external_id 590. The lookup now resolves only by the business keys (fid and external id), which are unique per merchant, so outlets always map to the correct merchant."
