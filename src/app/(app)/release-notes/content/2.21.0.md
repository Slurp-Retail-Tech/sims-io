title: "Google Calendar sync for sales appointments"
summary: "Sales appointments now sync to Google Calendar — created, edited, canceled, and completed appointments all keep their calendar event up to date. Logging a Meeting activity on a lead can now create the sales appointment for you, prefilled from the lead's details."
new:
  - title: "Sales appointments on Google Calendar"
    detail: "When Google Calendar sync is enabled, creating a sales appointment adds a one-hour event to the calendar. Editing the appointment moves the event, and canceling or completing it updates the event's status in the description. Sales events go to the calendar configured by GOOGLE_CALENDAR_SALES_ID, falling back to the shared GOOGLE_CALENDAR_ID when not set."
  - title: "Create sales appointments from lead meetings"
    detail: "The Log activity dialog for leads now offers a 'Create sales appointment' toggle on Meeting activities (pre-checked for Scheduled meetings). It creates a pending sales appointment using the lead's customer and business details plus the meeting's date and location — which then appears on the Sales Appointment page and in Google Calendar."
