new:
  - title: "Lead detail view with deals and activity log"
    detail: "Each lead now opens a dedicated detail page with editable lead details, a list of deals (name, stage, amount, closed date), and a filterable activity log (Note, Email, Call, Task, Meeting, WhatsApp Message) with type-specific fields."
  - title: "Manual lead creation and assignment"
    detail: "Authorised users can create leads manually (labelled with the \"manual\" source) and assign leads to Sales & Marketing users. Sales & Marketing users see only the leads assigned to them; admins and super admins see all leads."
  - title: "Deals page (List & Kanban)"
    detail: "A new Deals page under Sales & Marketing lists every deal across leads and offers a Kanban board with one column per stage. Drag a deal card between columns to change its stage; moving a deal to Closed Won prompts for a closed date, and Closed Lost also prompts for a close lost reason."
improved:
  - title: "Canonical lead source labels"
    detail: "Lead sources are now standardised to web, mobile, and manual. Existing web demo-form leads previously labelled \"desktop\" or \"demo-form\" are migrated to \"web\"."
