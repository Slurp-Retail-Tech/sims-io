  - title: "Individual page access for Sales workspace"

  - title: "Individual page access for Renewal & Retention workspace"
    detail: "Renewal & Retention is now split into three individually grantable pages — Overview, Renewal Due, and Analytics — instead of a single workspace toggle."
  - title: "Group select-all shortcut preserved"
    detail: "Checking the workspace-level group label in the user management page still selects all pages under that workspace, so bulk grants remain a single click."
fixed:
  - title: "Phantom access keys removed"
    detail: "Stale entries for routes that no longer exist (e.g. /dashboard, /users, /settings, /renewals) were removed from the page access configuration. Users with those grants were unaffected."
  - title: "Page access groups restructured"
    detail: "The user management page now organises permissions under four clearly labelled departments: Merchant Success, Sales & Marketing, Renewal & Retention, and Operations."
