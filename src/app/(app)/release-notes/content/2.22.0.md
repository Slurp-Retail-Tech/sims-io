new:
  - title: "Meeting activity edits follow through to the sales appointment"
    detail: "A Meeting activity that created a sales appointment now stays linked to it. Rescheduling or moving the meeting updates the still-pending appointment and its calendar event; setting the outcome to Canceled, changing the activity type, or deleting the activity cancels the appointment. Completed or already-canceled appointments are never touched."
  - title: "Google Maps location picker for sales meetings"
    detail: "The lead Meeting activity dialog and the Sales Appointment form now use the same Google Maps location search as onboarding scheduling. Picked places store the map link and coordinates, and the calendar event description includes a Google Maps link. Free-text locations still work when Places lookup is disabled."
  - title: "Calendar invites and Google Meet links"
    detail: "Sales calendar events now invite the appointment creator, and Online meetings accept comma-separated participant emails (prefilled with the lead's email). Participants receive the calendar invite, and Online events get an automatic Google Meet link that is stored and shown on the Sales Appointment page."
